Annual Events

Six annual celebrations of Indian culture, heritage, and community in Greater Tulsa.

Year Round

A Full Year of Celebration

IAGT hosts six annual events celebrating Indian culture, heritage, and community. From Republic Day to New Year's Eve, there's always something to look forward to.

🎆
January 26
Republic Day Celebration & GBM

Honoring India's Republic Day with a cultural ceremony and General Body Meeting. A proud celebration of India's constitutional legacy with the Tulsa community.

🎤
Coming Soon
Karaoke Night

A fun-filled evening of music, singing, and community bonding. Open to all IAGT members and friends.

🌈
Spring
Holi Celebration

A joyful celebration of the festival of colors — featuring music, vibrant colors, traditional sweets, and spring festivities for all ages.

🎊
August
Utsav — India Fest

Our flagship annual festival — FREE and open to the public! Exciting dances, Indian culture, cuisine, art, and more all in one place.

🪔
October
Diwali Celebration

The festival of lights! Performances, fireworks, and community gathering. The annual Executive Committee election results are announced at this event.

🥂
December 31
New Year's Eve Party

Ring in the New Year with dinner, dancing, and our vibrant IAGT community. A beloved tradition for members and families.
