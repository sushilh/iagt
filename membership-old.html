Benefits

What You Get

Your membership directly supports cultural programming, scholarships, and community initiatives across Greater Tulsa.

How to Pay

Pay via PayPal using the buttons above, mail a check payable to IAGT to:
P.O. Box 471802, Tulsa, OK 74147, or pay in person at any annual event. You can also use PayPal: @IAGTNYE2020

Annual membership runs Sept 1 – Aug 31. Members are encouraged to renew early to get full benefits.

  • Free entrance to all annual IAGT events
  • Exclusive opportunity for children and family to perform at IAGT events
  • Kids become eligible to apply for IAGT Scholarship when applying for colleges
  • Eligible to serve on IAGT committee — build leadership and organizational skills
  • Voting rights in annual elections (one vote per Single, max two per Family)
  • Access to Membership Directory (one per household per year)
  • Community newsletter and announcements
  • Network with 500+ Indian-American families in Greater Tulsa
