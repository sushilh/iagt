Who We Are

About IAGT

A nonprofit built on culture, community, and service in Greater Tulsa — since 1972.

Mission

What We Stand For

The India Association of Greater Tulsa is a nonprofit dedicated to promoting unity, cultural awareness, and community engagement among people of Indian origin and the broader Tulsa community. Founded with a spirit of service, IAGT has been a cornerstone of Indian-American life in Oklahoma for over 50 years.
