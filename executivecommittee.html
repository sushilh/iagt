Executive Committee

The team responsible for the day-to-day administration and events of IAGT.

About the Committee

Role & Responsibilities

The Executive Committee organizes the activities of IAGT and is responsible for the day-to-day monitoring and administration of the Association. The committee has a minimum of nine (9) and maximum of thirteen (13) members.

Each member serves a two-year term with a maximum of two consecutive terms. Elections are held annually, one hour before the Diwali function each fall.

🏛
President

Heads the Executive Committee, serves as Board member, liaises with Embassy and Consulates of India, and leads the Association with conviction and fairness.

📋
Secretary / Joint Secretary

Records minutes, distributes meeting notices, prepares annual reports, and maintains the IAGT member list.

💰
Treasurer / Joint Treasurer

Manages financial records, files tax returns, oversees bank accounts, and prepares quarterly financial reports and annual budget.

🎭
Cultural Events Officers

Coordinate the organization and execution of all IAGT cultural events throughout the year.
