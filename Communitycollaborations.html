Beyond Our Events

Building Bridges

IAGT actively collaborates with schools, civic organizations, and cultural institutions across Tulsa to promote diversity, inclusion, and a deeper understanding of Indian culture.

🏫
Eisenhower International Cultural Fair

Celebrating cultural diversity through student-led exhibits and performances at Eisenhower International School.

🌿
Desi Mela at Guthrie Green — SAPAF

A vibrant South Asian cultural festival held at Guthrie Green, co-organized with SAPAF.

🌍
Global Gatherings — Gathering Place

Connecting diverse Tulsa communities through shared cultural experiences at the Gathering Place.

🧵
Woven Identities: Global Threads, Local Stories

An exhibition partnership celebrating the stories and identities of Tulsa's global residents.

🤝
Welcoming Week Kickoff — Gathering Place

Hosted by Welcoming America and City of Tulsa — IAGT participates to welcome new community members.

🏮
Asian American Night Market — Tulsa Global District

Showcasing Asian-American culture, food, and art in the heart of Tulsa's Global District.

🏛
Asian Affairs Commission

Collaborating with the Asian Affairs Commission to address community needs and cultural representation.

🎒
Schools, Girl Scouts & Community Organizations

IAGT participates in events requested by schools, Girl Scouts, and other organizations to share Indian culture.
