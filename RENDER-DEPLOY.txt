WHAT YOU NEED
─────────────
  • A free GitHub account  →  github.com
  • A free Render account  →  render.com
  • This website folder

════════════════════════════════════════
STEP 1 — Put files on GitHub
════════════════════════════════════════

1. Go to github.com and sign in (or create free account)

2. Click the green "New" button to create a new repository
   • Name it:  iagt-website
   • Set to:   Public  (required for free Render hosting)
   • Click:    "Create repository"

3. Upload your files:
   • Click "uploading an existing file" link on the new repo page
   • Drag the entire iagt-website/ folder contents into the box
     (upload ALL files: index.html, about.html, css/, js/, fonts/)
   • Scroll down, click "Commit changes"

   ✅ Your files are now on GitHub!

════════════════════════════════════════
STEP 2 — Connect to Render
════════════════════════════════════════

4. Go to render.com and sign in (or create free account)

5. Click "New +" button in the top right

6. Select "Static Site" from the dropdown

7. Click "Connect GitHub" and authorize Render to access your account

8. Find and select your "iagt-website" repository, click "Connect"

════════════════════════════════════════
STEP 3 — Configure the deployment
════════════════════════════════════════

9. Fill in these settings:
   ┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
   │  Name:              iagt-website                    │
   │  Branch:            main                            │
   │  Root Directory:    (leave blank)                   │
   │  Build Command:     (leave blank)                   │
   │  Publish Directory: .          ← just a dot        │
   └─────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

10. Click "Create Static Site"

════════════════════════════════════════
STEP 4 — Your site is live!
════════════════════════════════════════

11. Render will build and deploy in about 30–60 seconds

12. Your site will be live at:
      https://iagt-website.onrender.com
    (or similar — Render shows you the URL)

13. Click the URL to see your live website! 🎉

════════════════════════════════════════
STEP 5 — Add your custom domain (Optional)
════════════════════════════════════════

To use iagtok.org instead of the .onrender.com URL:

14. In Render dashboard, go to your site → Settings → Custom Domains

15. Click "Add Custom Domain" and type:  iagtok.org

16. Render will show you DNS settings to add:
      Type:   CNAME
      Name:   www
      Value:  iagt-website.onrender.com

17. Log into your domain registrar (GoDaddy, Namecheap, etc.)
    and add those DNS records

18. Wait 5–60 minutes for DNS to propagate — then your site
    will be live at iagtok.org with free HTTPS! ✅

════════════════════════════════════════
FUTURE UPDATES — Automatic!
════════════════════════════════════════

After setup, updating the site is simple:

  → Edit a file → upload to GitHub → Render auto-deploys in ~30 seconds

No manual rebuilding. No servers to manage. It's all automatic.
