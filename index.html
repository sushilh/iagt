Our Mission

A Community
Built on Heritage

The India Association of Greater Tulsa is a nonprofit dedicated to promoting unity, cultural awareness, and community engagement among people of Indian origin and the broader Tulsa community.

Through festivals, scholarships, and civic engagement, we preserve the richness of Indian culture while building lasting bridges across our communities.
